How to Get a Google App Password

An App Password lets RetailEdge AI connect to your Gmail account securely via IMAP to automatically import invoice attachments.

⚠️ Before You Start

  • You need a Google account (Gmail or Google Workspace)
  • 2-Step Verification must be enabled on the account (we'll show you how)
  • The email account should be the one that receives your supplier invoices — it doesn't need to match your RetailEdge login email
1
Enable 2-Step Verification
Required before you can create App Passwords

If you already have 2-Step Verification enabled, skip to Step 2.

  1. Go to myaccount.google.com/security
  2. Sign in with the Gmail account that receives your invoices
  3. Scroll down to the "How you sign in to Google" section
  4. Click on "2-Step Verification"
  5. Follow the prompts to set up verification (phone number or authenticator app)

Generate Your App Password
Create a 16-character password for RetailEdge AI
  1. In the "App name" field, type RetailEdge AI (or any name you'll remember)
  2. Click Create
  3. Google will display a 16-character password — this is your App Password

4
Verify IMAP is Enabled
Gmail needs IMAP turned on (usually enabled by default)
  1. Open Gmail in your browser
  2. Click the gear icon (top right) → See all settings
  3. Go to the "Forwarding and POP/IMAP" tab
  4. Under IMAP access, make sure "Enable IMAP" is selected
  5. Click Save Changes if you made any changes

5
Enter Credentials in RetailEdge AI
Go back to the Connect Wizard and complete setup
  1. Go back to the RetailEdge AI Connect Wizard
  2. Select Gmail → Simple Setup (App Password)
  3. Enter your Gmail address (the one that receives invoices)
  4. Paste your 16-character App Password
  5. Configure your import settings (sender whitelist, poll interval)
  6. Click Test Connection to verify everything works
  7. Click Complete Setup — you're done!
🎉
That's it! RetailEdge AI will now automatically monitor your Gmail inbox for invoice attachments and process them through the AI pipeline. PDFs, images, and scanned documents are all supported.
Frequently Asked Questions
Can I use a different Gmail account than my login email?
Yes! The Gmail account used for invoice import is completely separate from your RetailEdge AI login email. Use whichever Gmail receives your supplier invoices.
Is my App Password stored securely?
Yes. Your App Password is encrypted using AES-256-GCM encryption before being stored in the database. We never store plaintext credentials.
Can I revoke access later?
Yes. Go to myaccount.google.com/apppasswords and delete the App Password for RetailEdge AI. This immediately stops RetailEdge from accessing your Gmail.
What's the difference between Simple Setup and Advanced Setup?
Simple Setup uses an App Password via IMAP — quick and easy. Advanced Setup uses Google OAuth, which provides more granular permission control but requires setting up a Google Cloud project. Both methods achieve the same result: automatic invoice import from Gmail.
My organization uses Google Workspace. Can I still use App Passwords?
It depends on your organization's admin settings. Your Google Workspace admin may need to enable "Allow users to manage their App Passwords" in the Admin Console under Security settings. Contact your IT admin if you can't find the App Passwords option.
I lost my App Password. What do I do?
No problem — just go back to myaccount.google.com/apppasswords and create a new one. Then update it in RetailEdge AI by going through the Gmail setup wizard again.
